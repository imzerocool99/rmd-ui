How Agentic AI Transforms RMD Processing
From manual, error-prone operations to autonomous, tax-efficient, client-centric execution
❌ Without the Agent (Manual Process)
  • Advisor manually calculates RMD amount each year
  • Random or gut-feel asset selection for liquidation
  • No tax optimization — often triggers unnecessary gains
  • Manual trade tickets — slow, error-prone
  • No explanation given to client on asset selection
  • Risk of missing IRS deadline → 25% excise tax penalty
  • Cannot scale without hiring more operations staff
✅ With the Intelligent RMD Agent
  • Agent auto-identifies eligible accounts and calculates RMD
  • AI scores 31 assets across 10 classes for tax efficiency
  • Liquidates losers first — minimizes tax drag automatically
  • Executes trades via Alpaca API — no manual ticket
  • AI generates plain-English explanation for every decision
  • Zero missed deadlines — scheduled agent runs year-round
  • Scales to thousands of accounts with no added staff
