What Each Setting Does
Temperature
0.1–0.3 — Best for financial advice. Responses are consistent and factual. Recommended for production.
0.5–0.7 — More variation in phrasing. Good for client-facing summaries.
0.8+ — Creative but may introduce inaccuracies. Avoid for financial content.
Max Tokens
Controls how long the response can be. 256 tokens ≈ a short paragraph. 512 tokens ≈ 2 paragraphs. Higher means slower response time.
Model
phi3 — Fast, runs on CPU, good for simple Q&A.
llama3.1 — Much better reasoning, needs GPU or fast CPU.
mistral — Good balance of speed and quality.
