Cash Distribution

📦
In-Kind Transfer
Moves shares from IRA to taxable brokerage at FMV per IRS Pub. 590-B. Client stays invested while satisfying the RMD requirement.
🎗️
QCD Donate
Transfers assets tax-free directly from IRA to charity. $0 income recognized — most tax-efficient strategy for charitably inclined clients aged 70½+.
⏸
Hold
Excludes an asset from this RMD cycle. Remaining holdings cover the full RMD. Advisor can hold high-conviction positions without disrupting the plan.
🤖
AI Explanation
Every decision explained in plain English with clickable IRS source citations. Client understands exactly why each asset was selected or protected.
✍️
Digital Authorization
